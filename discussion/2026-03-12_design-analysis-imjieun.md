## Design Elements

### Color System
- **Primary accent**: Orange (#E8734A) — used for hook slide bg, avatar, highlights
- **Content background**: Dark/near-black (#1A1A1A)
- **Text**: White on dark, black on light
- **Highlight**: Fluorescent green for key terms
- **Category dots**: 🔴 Red, 📖 Orange, 🏗 Blue, ✅ Green

### Typography
- **Titles**: Extra large bold Korean (approx 48-60px equivalent)
- **Body**: Medium regular (approx 18-24px)
- **Code/terminal**: Monospace font

### Layout Components
- **Series tag pill**: Top of every slide, rounded rectangle
- **Question pill**: Gray/dark rounded button with question text
- **Comparison tables**: Clean 2-3 column layout
- **Terminal mockup**: macOS-style window with colored dots (red/yellow/green)
- **Avatar**: Orange character with chef hat (personal branding)
- **Author credit**: Bottom of slide 1, "@handle" on CTA slide

### Key Design Principles
1. **Consistent series branding** across all slides
2. **Question-driven** content flow (each slide answers a question)
3. **High contrast** (white text on dark bg)
4. **Code/terminal aesthetics** fit the tech audience
5. **One idea per slide** — never crowded
6. **Hook → Content → CTA** standard funnel
